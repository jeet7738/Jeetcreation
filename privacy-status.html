Privacy Policy for Save Status Pro

Last Updated: July 11, 2025

Thank you for using Save Status Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Save Status Pro is designed to respect your privacy. The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To access and display media files on your device.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save media files to your device.
  • INTERNET: To display advertisements (if applicable).
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check network connectivity for ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • Media files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.
  • We use third-party services like Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect anonymous usage data.

3. Data Security

All media processing happens locally on your device. We do not transmit your files or any personal data to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Their privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and updates.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jeetcreation03@gmail.com.

By using Save Status Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
